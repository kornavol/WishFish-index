Team

Why we created this service

When I was a boy, I loved to congratulate my mother on holidays. Touching drawing of postcards with pencils by hand. As time went on. Homemade cards gradually gave way to thematic purchased ones. Then in the daily cycle of life, the gift of cards turned into verbal congratulations on the phone, and then even in SMS. Family worries were constantly distracting. I often forgot to send my congratulations on time. Last year my mother was gone. This moment was marked by the realization that I would never be able to catch up. That's when I decided to make this online service of heartfelt sincere congratulations. So that at any moment, even when loved ones are far from each other, the flame of love between close people does not go out

Andreas

Developer

Have you ever been a victim of a mailing list? Yes? And not from online store service, but from a close friend! These terrible feelings of equalization of all in an indiscriminate way. I felt like not being a friend, but as a match in a matchbox, to whom congratulations are written on duty, for show. Don't you have enough imagination, I thought, to write a personal greeting? Or maybe a trivial lack of time issue? At this instant, I realized that I wanted to help people create exclusive greetings for their close ones without wasting time and with pleasure.

That was the first time I realized how important it is to put sincere feelings and emotions into your message.
